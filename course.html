COACHING FOR KIDS

Interactive, lively and affordable coaching seesions for kids

🌞📖

Science

This course fosters curiosity and critical thinking as students explore Biology, Chemistry, Earth and Space Science, and Physics. Through hands-on experiments and real-world applications, learners build scientific skills and understanding.
The program encourages inquiry, creativity, and problem-solving, laying the foundation for scientific literacy and a lifelong love of discovery.

Core Areas

             🧬 Biology
             🧪 Chemistry
             🌎 Earth & Space Science
             ⚖️ Physics


Math

This course builds essential skills in arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and problem-solving. Students will explore mathematical concepts through real-world applications, hands-on activities, and collaborative learning. The course aims to develop critical thinking, confidence, and a strong foundation for future studies in math and related fields.

Core Areas

             🔟 Number & Algebra
             📐 Measurement & Geometry
             📈 Statistics & Probability
             🧮 Mathematical Reasoning


ICT

This course introduces students to essential digital skills through hands-on activities in areas like coding, multimedia, online safety, and digital communication. Students learn to think critically, solve problems, and use technology responsibly and creatively. The course builds confidence in using a range of digital tools, preparing students for future learning and careers.

Core Areas

             🖥️ Digital Literacy
             🛡️ Cyber Safety & Digital Citezenship
             👩‍💻 Programming & Coding
             📹 Multimedia & Design
             🤖 Emerging Technologies
